A group project for desiging an underwater vehicle for industrial needs. This bio-inspired design combines the capabilities of a conventional ROV with perks of AUVs. Some notable features of this design project:

  • Detailed CAD design
  • Modular and robust design
  • Multi-purpose design with high-end sensors
  • Simulated and hydrostatic and hydrodynamic analysis using ANSYS
  • Diffrent Control Modes for various underwater opperations with increased stability and maneuverability
