A joint research project about swarms coordination algorithm that can models deformable-body movement in 2D & also avoiding obstacles while moving towards a target, during an internship at M&MoCS research center in Italy.

  • Lagrangian modeling of the swarm
  • Developing an obstacle avoidance algorithm
  • Investigating the Poisson effect on the stability of the swarm
  • Autonomous trajectory planning in a field of obstacles
  • Coding and simulation using Wolfram Mathematica
