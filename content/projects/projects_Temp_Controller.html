This project was carried out during the “Embedded Systems” hands-on course to design an electronic circuit and programming an ARM microcontroller to control a temperature chamber. The following topics have been covered during this course:

  • ARM Microcontrollers Architecture
  • Design Circuits for Power Supply - Linear/Switching Regulators
  • Design Circuits for Digital InputOutput
  • Design Circuits for Analog Input/Output - Protection Circuit
  • Keypad and LCD Interfacing
  • RS485 Communication Interface
  • STM32 Programming - GPIO, DMA, ADC, USART, Interrupts
  • LCD and Keypad Interface Programming
  • HMI Communication - Modbus Protocol
