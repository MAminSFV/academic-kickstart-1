Modeling and Motion Control of a redundant manipulator, on kinematic Level, with 6 DOFs and 3-dimensional task space, tracking specific spatial trajectories.

  • Direct kinematics and Jacobian analysis
  • Simulating kinematics of the robot using Matlab and Simulink
  • Using live-link connection with V-REP to visualize the Robot and its end-effector trajectory
  • Inverse kinematic controller, using Jacobian pseudo-inverse with active null space
  • Programmed trajectories: rectilinear, circular, and helix
